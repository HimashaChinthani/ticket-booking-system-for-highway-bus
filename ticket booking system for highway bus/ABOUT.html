About us

BusSeat.lk, developed by Techkitez (Pvt) Ltd, is an innovative online bus ticket booking platform aimed at enhancing public transportation in Sri Lanka. Since its establishment in 2015, BusSeat.lk has revolutionized the bus ticketing industry by providing a user-friendly interface for passengers to reserve seats and efficiently manage records for bus companies and regulatory bodies.
Traditionally, bus transportation in Sri Lanka has been manual, leading to inefficiencies and backlogs.
BusSeat.lk addresses this by offering an all-encompassing system that streamlines scheduling, bookings, and individual bus data. The platform has received positive feedback from over 100,000 users, highlighting its efficiency and convenience. With the tourism industry in Sri Lanka growing rapidly, an efficient transport system is essential.

BusSeat.lk caters to both locals and tourists, providing a seamless booking
experience for travelers exploring the island's attractions. By offering pre-booking options, the platform assists tourists in planning their journeys in advance, contributing to a memorable travel experience. As Sri Lanka aims to develop its infrastructure, BusSeat.lk plays a crucial role in advancing the country's transport and technological capabilities. With services covering almost all tourism hotspots, the platform facilitates easy access to transportation for tourists, aligning with Sri Lanka's emergence as a prominent destination in South Asia.
